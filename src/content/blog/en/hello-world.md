# Welcome to MontageSubs Community

MontageSubs Community is officially launched.

We are a nonprofit open-source community dedicated to connecting audiences, translators, developers, and filmmakers to build an open, transparent subtitle ecosystem.

## Who We Are

We are not a traditional subtitle group — we think of ourselves more as builders of the subtitle ecosystem. Our focus extends beyond translation itself to include:

- **Tools**: Making subtitle creation more efficient and standardized
- **Research**: Exploring translation theory, localization strategies, and technical standards
- **Community**: Creating an open, collaborative space where subtitle enthusiasts can meet

## Our Mission

Good subtitles are more than translation — they are a bridge connecting different cultures, a medium that allows more people to experience the magic of film and television.

We believe subtitle creation should be **open, collaborative, and democratic**. We are dedicated to:

1. Lowering the technical barriers to subtitle creation
2. Raising the quality standards of subtitle translation
3. Building an open-source tool ecosystem
4. Facilitating exchange between translators, developers, and viewers

## What's Next

- Tool library under construction — first tools launching soon
- Translation notes series coming soon
- Technical standards documentation in progress
- Welcome to join our [Telegram](https://t.me/MontageSubs) and [Discord](https://discord.gg/montagesubs) communities

Thank you for your attention. We look forward to building this community with you.
